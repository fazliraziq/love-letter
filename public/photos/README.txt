PUT YOUR PHOTOS HERE
====================

Replace these placeholder images with your own. The easiest way:
keep the same file names, and your photos will appear automatically.

  hero.jpg      -> the big background photo on the opening screen
  photo-1.jpg   -> gallery photo 1   (wide)
  photo-2.jpg   -> gallery photo 2   (tall / portrait)
  photo-3.jpg   -> gallery photo 3   (tall / portrait)
  photo-4.jpg   -> gallery photo 4   (wide)
  photo-5.jpg   -> gallery photo 5
  photo-6.jpg   -> gallery photo 6

Tips
- JPG or PNG both work. If you use PNG, rename it to end in .jpg
  OR update the file name in src/config.js.
- Landscape (wide) photos look best for hero, photo-1 and photo-4.
- Portrait (tall) photos look best for photo-2 and photo-3.
- Big, high-resolution photos look great. ~2000px wide is plenty.
